Work, Power & Energy

Work: In physics, work is related to the amount of energy transferred in or from a system by a force. It is a scalar-valued quantity with SI units of Joule.

Power: Power is defined to be the rate at which work is performed, or the derivative of work over time. The SI unit for power is the watt. OR: Rate of doing work with respect to time is called power

Energy: Energy is stored work. It has the same units as work, the Joule (J).There are many forms of energy:Spring energy,Gravitational potential energy, Kinetic energy, Electrical energy, Photon energy, Chemical energy and Nuclear energy
